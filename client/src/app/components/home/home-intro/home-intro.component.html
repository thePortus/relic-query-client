Introduction A Background

Welcome to RelicQuery. This is a portfolio demostration project, and was built in less than a week. This app is designed to take uploads from users, store them on a server, and allow others to download their models and even view them in the browser. App only accept uploads from users who have been approved by the site owner as editors.

Currently all models were either modeled or scanned by myself.

This project is for demostration purposes only!

Click the navigation button in the top-right and head to "Models" to get started!
